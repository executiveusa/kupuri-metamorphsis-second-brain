Use the provided circular logo image as the final frame reference: a glowing chrysalis over a lotus, morpho-blue butterfly identity, gold circular frame, soft teal-blue and warm gold background.

## Scene

3.5–5.5s: Blue morpho butterfly wings slowly emerge from the opening chrysalis. The movement is graceful and realistic, like a high-end animated film. The wings unfold in slow motion with iridescent cyan, deep blue, and subtle gold vein detail.

5.5–6.8s: The butterfly fully emerges, beats its wings once, and flies gently toward the camera. It leaves a trail of teal and gold particles.

## Style

Cinematic realistic cartoon. Premium wellness. Mystic clarity. Not childish. Not medical. Not generic. Use realistic lighting, soft glow, high detail, gentle motion, and a calm emotional tone.

## Constraints

No extra text besides `Entrar`. No random butterflies. Do not alter the locked logo colors. Keep the circular frame composition. Make the final frame loop-friendly.
