# PRODUCT TRUTH — Metamorphosis

## Non-negotiable product identity

Metamorphosis is **Ivette Milo’s private wellness second brain and personal health tracker first**.

It is not a public SaaS first.  
It is not a generic wellness landing page.  
It is not a pricing funnel.  
It is not a menopause app.  
It is not a content subscription.  
It is not a place for fake dashboards or hard-coded product theater.

Every feature must help Ivette do at least one of these things:

- track her wellness
- remember her health and nutrition context
- capture reflections
- create meals and recipes
- plan meals for the week
- listen to music/audio for state regulation
- build better daily rituals
- search her second brain
- export or back up her personal data
- let trusted agents safely help her

## Product sentence

Metamorphosis is Ivette’s private wellness operating system: a calm personal lab for journals, rituals, recipes, meal plans, audio, nutrition memory, and second-brain search.

## Public product rule

The public product can come later only after the private lab works for Ivette in real daily use.

Until then:

- no pricing pages
- no subscription tiers
- no trial language
- no fake admin console
- no generic programs
- no investor-facing product bloat

## Builder rule

If a proposed feature does not directly improve Ivette’s daily private use, it goes into `docs/future-platform/` as an idea, not production code.
